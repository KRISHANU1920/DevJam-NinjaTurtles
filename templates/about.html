About GyaanSangrah

GyaanSangrah is a one and only website related to education in which we provide the students - details about the academics and how to prepare for Internship/Placement. In this website we have tried to provide with all the best possible study materials and resources related to Academics and Placements in a well planned manner. The Academics section comprises of Books, Previous Year Questions, Assignments, Time Tables and Calendars for easily cracking the semester exams with descent GPA. The Placement section comprises of Tutorial and Practice Questions of various Data Structures and Algorithms, Competitive Programming, and Core Subjects. It also consist of awesome Project Ideas and Resume Building Tips and Sites!
